What we do

WALLET

More than 2 billion of the world’s people are unbanked, primarily due to a lack of access and high costs. At TantaPay, our mission is to get the entire world access to financial services.

If you are a foreign worker looking to send money home to your family, a world traveler looking for the easiest way to get foreign currency, a small business owner looking to expand, or an ambitious saver looking for a place to put your savings to work, we have a solution for you.

TRANSFERS

TantaPay believes payments should be fast, easy, and secure, no matter where you are. Send money to your family overseas instantly, and without big transaction fees. Pay your friends, your taxi driver, or your favorite restaurant instantly, securely, and without the need for a bulky wallet.

FOREIGN EXCHANGE

Change currencies when you’re traveling without the hassle or fees of a Foreign Exchange bureau. Exchange as much or as little money as you want, when you want, at current market rates, without exchange fees. When you’re home, change back to your local currency with the same ease and convenience, or keep the foreign currency until your next trip.

LEND

No matter how much money you have today, we believe that everyone should have an opportunity to build wealth. At TantaPay you can start building your wealth today by lending to other TantaPay users based on your time horizon and risk tolerance, putting your financial future in your own hands.

BORROW

At TantaPay you can build a fair credit score based on your activity, not someone else’s metrics, allowing you to borrow money based on your credit worthiness, not the value of assets you can use as collateral. We allow the market to set the loan terms, so you can know you are getting the best possible rate. As you build credit, you can borrow larger amounts of money on better terms, creating a virtuous cycle of growth for your business.
